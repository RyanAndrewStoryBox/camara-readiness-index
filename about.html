About the CAMARA Readiness Index

The telecom network API market is growing fast. Dozens of operators, aggregators, and platform providers now claim CAMARA or Open Gateway support. But claims and readiness are different things.

Developers want to know which providers have usable documentation. Buyers want to know who publishes pricing. Product teams want to know where a sandbox exists. The answers are scattered across press releases, developer portals, and conference slides. Nobody has pulled them into one place with a consistent scoring method.

The CAMARA Readiness Index does that. We assess each provider's public-facing API surface using a 7-category rubric, score it 0 to 5, and publish the results with full methodology and evidence trails.

Oriel, a digital agency, maintains this index. Scores come from structured assessment against public evidence. No provider pays for placement or influence over their score.

Who uses this

Developers and solution architects evaluating telecom API options. Product and innovation teams comparing providers. Enterprise buyers and procurement teams running shortlists. Operators and aggregators benchmarking their own public surfaces. Analysts and investors tracking market maturity.

This is a beta

The Readiness Index is in active development. The scoring model, provider coverage, and site features will evolve based on feedback and market changes. If you spot an error, disagree with a score, or want to suggest a provider for assessment, we want to hear from you.
